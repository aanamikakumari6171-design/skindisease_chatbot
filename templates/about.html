About SkinCheck AI

SkinCheck AI is an AI-powered web application designed to help users detect possible skin diseases within seconds. It combines cutting-edge machine learning models with an easy-to-use interface.

Key Features:

  • Upload an image of affected skin for instant analysis
  • Describe symptoms in text for AI-based detection
  • Voice input available for hands-free interaction
  • Personalized result page with probable disease, symptoms & suggestions
  • Chatbot assistance for step-by-step guidance
  • Modern, simple, and user-friendly interface

How It Works:

Our deep learning model has been trained on thousands of skin disease images. Once a user uploads an image or describes their condition, the AI analyzes the input, compares it with its trained data, and returns the most probable skin condition along with guidance.

Why Choose SkinCheck AI?

  • Quick detection in just a few seconds
  • Helps spread awareness about skin health
  • Supports multiple input modes (Image, Text, Voice)
  • Completely free and accessible
